Teuchter
Manifesto
  1. Our shows explore the individual identities of this present moment, born from globalisation and multiculturalism.
  2. We work together across disciplines and genres. We explore the lines between artistic genres. We are influenced by each other.
  3. We resist the orthodoxy that art ‘makes’ audiences laugh, cry, or feel anything at all. We never tell you how to feel. We give you the opportunity to feel, or dream, or imagine things.
  4. We generate original work. We avoid adaptations. We are careful with our nostalgia.
  5. We are committed to inclusivity. We resist the closed loops of our Facebook feeds.
  6. We don’t know more or better than our audiences.

- Teuchter Theater Company
